FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to be local to Columbus to work with you?
No. We're physically in Clintonville (Columbus, OH 43214), but we serve clients across the country through a secure document portal and video meetings. About a third of our clients are remote.
How early should I send my tax documents?
Mid-February through mid-March is the sweet spot for personal returns. Business returns with a March 15 deadline should be underway by mid-February. We'll send you a prepared checklist the moment you're onboarded.
What software do you use?
Professional-grade: CCH Axcess and Drake for returns, a secure Intuit Link / SafeSend portal for documents, and QuickBooks Online or Xero for bookkeeping engagements. Everything is encrypted in transit and at rest.
I have a notice from the IRS — can you help today?
Yes. Send it over through the contact form and we'll respond the same business day. Most notices have a response window of 30 to 90 days; responding early (and correctly) is almost always what saves money.
Do you do crypto?
Yes — we handle crypto reporting for traders, miners, and long-term holders. We prefer reconciled exports from Koinly, CoinTracker or similar; if you don't have those yet, we'll help you get them.
