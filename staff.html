Founder

Wesley Stanovsek, CPA

Wesley founded Noosa to bring a big-firm depth of technical skill to the neighborhood-firm experience he wished his family had growing up.

Wesley earned his Bachelor of Business Administration in Accounting at The Ohio State University's Fisher College of Business, then sharpened his tax chops across three very different seats: as a tax preparer at Crowe, a senior associate at GHJ in Los Angeles working on S-corps, partnerships and trusts for high-net-worth clients, and — most recently — as a Revenue Agent in the IRS's High Wealth division. That means your return is prepared by someone who understands how the IRS actually reads it.

Outside of work, Wesley is usually at a coffee shop in Clintonville, running along the Olentangy Trail, or at a Buckeyes game.

How we work

Four quiet commitments.

Plain English

If you can't explain what's on your return, we haven't done our job. Every deliverable comes with a one-page summary.

On time, always

If we can't meet a deadline, we tell you early and we file the extension for free. We don't hide surprises.

Clear pricing

Flat fees, quoted in writing before we start. If a return turns out to be simpler than estimated, we adjust.

Security, seriously

Encrypted portal, enforced MFA, and a written data-retention policy. We never email sensitive PDFs.
